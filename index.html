General Instructions

📚 Attendance & Discipline:
- Students must attend all scheduled classes on time.
- Late arrivals may be marked as absent unless justified.
- Attendance will directly impact academic performance.

📝 Assignments & Study:
- Homework must be submitted before the deadline.
- Students are encouraged to review lessons daily.
- Group study is allowed but plagiarism is not tolerated.

📅 Exams:
- All students must be present during exam days.
- Bring necessary materials (pens, calculators, etc.).
- Cheating will result in disciplinary action.

💻 Online System Usage:
- Use your school account to log in to the system.
- Do not share your password with others.
- If you find any issue, report it to the IT supervisor.

🤝 Behavior & Respect:
- Respect your teachers, classmates, and school property.
- Any form of bullying or discrimination is strictly prohibited.
- Dress code must be followed at all times.

📞 Communication & Support:
- For academic help, contact your subject teacher.
- For schedule or system issues, contact the school office.
- Parents can contact administration for urgent matters.
